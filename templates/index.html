How To Get App Password

  1. Open Gmail, then click your profile photo and choose Manage your Google Account.
  2. Open the Security tab and make sure 2-Step Verification is enabled.
  3. Use the account search bar or the Security page search and type App Passwords.
  4. Open App Passwords, choose a mail/app label, and generate the 16-character password.
  5. Paste that app password here with your Gmail address, upload email.csv, add your subject and message, then send.
